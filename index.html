Who We Are

At PENTACHIP, we are pioneers in the design and development of Smart Battery Management Systems (BMS). Founded by a passionate team of engineers and innovators, our company is driven by the mission to power a sustainable and smarter future through intelligent energy solutions.

Headquartered in Trivandrum, India, we combine engineering excellence with deep industry insight to deliver high-performance BMS technology tailored for a wide range of applications from electric vehicles to energy storage systems. Our products are built on precision, safety, and reliability, making us a trusted partner in the global clean energy ecosystem.

What We Do

At PENTACHIP, we design and develop Smart Battery Management Systems (BMS) that power the future of sustainable energy. Our BMS solutions are engineered for reliability, efficiency, and intelligent performance ensuring that every battery operates at its full potential, safely and effectively.

PENTACHIP BMS units come with integrated sensors and smart algorithms that offer real-time diagnostics, predictive maintenance, and wireless monitoring giving users complete visibility and control over their energy systems.

Every application is unique. That's why we also offer customized BMS designs tailored to meet specific requirements of OEMs, startups, and energy innovators.

We're not just building products we're creating future-ready technology. From AI-driven energy optimization to IoT-enabled battery ecosystems, we are constantly pushing boundaries to deliver smarter, safer, and more efficient energy solutions.
